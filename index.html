Our Story

TWICE THE
FLAVOR

Thanksgiving 2013. Two brothers-in-law, a kitchen, and a homebrew kit that changed everything. Brendan Byrne and David Cristelot discovered a shared obsession that night—the deep, roasted complexity of dark beers. Porters. Stouts. The kind of craft that rewards patience and punishes shortcuts.

Ten years of relentless refinement followed. Competitions entered and won. Recipes that started as curiosity became something worth building a life around. David stepped away from finance. Brendan from IT. Both took a second chance on something that actually mattered.

In September 2023, Take Two opened its doors on Union Boulevard—steps from the Bay Shore LIRR station. The name carries a double meaning: a second career for its founders, and the conviction that one great beer is just never enough.

Community

ST. BALDRICK'S
FUNDRAISER

Every year, Take Two hosts a head-shaving fundraiser for the St. Baldrick's Foundation — the nation's largest private funder of childhood cancer research grants. Participants brave the shave, the community rallies, and every dollar goes straight to funding research that saves kids' lives.

Every 2 minutes a child is diagnosed with cancer. This event is our way of fighting back — one shaved head, one pint, one donation at a time.

Co-founder David Cristelot and the Association of Long Island Homebrew Clubs lead the charge as top fundraisers year after year. The brewery also brews a special SMaSH Kids Cancer Pale Ale for the event, available on tap while it lasts.
