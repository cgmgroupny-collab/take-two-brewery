Our Story

TWICE THE
FLAVOR

Thanksgiving 2013. Two brothers-in-law, a kitchen, and a homebrew kit that changed everything. Brendan Byrne and David Cristelot discovered a shared obsession that night—the deep, roasted complexity of dark beers. Porters. Stouts. The kind of craft that rewards patience and punishes shortcuts.

Ten years of relentless refinement followed. Living rooms converted to makeshift brewhouses. Competitions entered and won. Recipes that started as curiosity became something worth building a life around. David stepped away from finance. Brendan from IT. Both took a second chance on something that actually mattered.

In September 2023, Take Two opened its doors on Union Boulevard—steps from the Bay Shore LIRR station. The name carries a double meaning: a second career for its founders, and the conviction that one great beer is just never enough.
